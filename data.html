    Leed Data Trends From 2017 To 2021

    How has the sustainable built environment changed over the past five years? What does the data tell us about the future of green building? Here's an overview of the current market landscape based on registered and certified LEED projects from 2017 to 2021

    LEED Certifications Around The Globe

    Certifications over the past five years can be found around the globe. The highest concentration of LEED projects certified can be found in the United States and Canada (74%).

    Global Green Building Projects In Square Feet

    China and India are next in line, having invested massively in LEED-certified projects in 2022. Here is a full breakdown of the total square feet of LEED-certified construction projects in 2022 by country.

    India has achieved 75% of the 'Green Building Footprint' target in 2020

    India has successfully achieved 7.17 billion square feet of 'Green Building Footprint', according to the recent data by the Indian Green Building Council.

    State Wise Data In India

    According to the United States Green Building Council, India ranks third globally when it comes to green buildings, only behind Mainland China and Canada, and the number is steadily increasing. Within India, Maharashtra tops the list of green building leaders, followed by Karnataka, Haryana, Tamil Nadu & Uttar Pradesh, according to the LEED (Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design) survey of GCB-India.
